**Evolution of Games** is a personal playground repository where I recreate the history of video games using **C++**.

The concept is simple: I am moving through the timeline of the gaming industry, implementing classic titles from scratch. The complexity of the projects grows alongside the "historical era" of the games I recreate.

> **Note:** These projects are written for fun, relaxation, and general practice. They represent a tribute to gaming history rather than a demonstration of cutting-edge engine architecture.
